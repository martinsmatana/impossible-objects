Creative and business concept of the studio.

This document describes the creative and business concept of an AI video production studio. We create short ad videos using generative AI for brands that want visually amazing social media content without the high cost of traditional production.

What we do

We don't make videos. We develop the way people look at products.

Our true skill is the creative re-interpretation of a product. Technology (AI models) changes every three months. Our creative system of thinking does not.

Technology can be replaced. A creative system cannot.

And we license that system to companies.

Basic principle

One impossible element in an otherwise normal world.

A real street, real people, real lighting — and one thing that doesn't belong. A croissant instead of a roof on a Parisian building. A giant ice cream being studied by a man in a suit. Broccoli instead of trees on a boulevard.

This is the Magritte principle: the more normal the surroundings are, the stronger the surreal element is. If there are too many impossible elements, the effect is lost because there is no contrast.

How it works

Change of scale = change of value.

We change the size of the product — and by doing that, we change the relationship between the person and the product.

When you look at something at a different scale, you see it differently. When you see it differently, you give it a different value.

A croissant costs 0 cents.
But a building that is a croissant? That is a monument.

We sell a change of perspective through a change of scale.

Product as an object of study.

FORMAT 04 / 04

Product as a landscape.

The product becomes part of the environment — broccoli instead of trees, ice cream instead of traffic lights, bread instead of a train. This turns the product into a world. It works best for products with iconic shapes: bottles, bags, shoes, perfume flacons.

Compliance as a Service

Legal safety and IP protection.

Our offer includes a package of services that guarantees legal safety and the protection of intellectual property beyond standard production. Designed for clients who need to follow ESG rules and EU legislation.

Licensing Certification
We use only "Enterprise-safe" AI tools where the provider takes legal responsibility for training data. The client receives direct legal indemnity in case of copyright disputes.
IP Validation
Full documentation of the "human-in-the-loop" process — storyboards, prompt development, post-production. Proves that AI was used as a tool under human direction, and the output is the client's own IP.
Content Credentials (C2PA)
Cryptographic metadata embedded in the master files — a digital "birth certificate" for every output. Transparency for consumers and compatibility with platforms like Meta, YouTube and TikTok.
Digital Notarization
SHA-256 hash of the final video recorded in the delivery protocol. Protection against deepfakes and unauthorized edits. If your ad is manipulated, you can instantly prove which file is the original.
EU AI Act Audit
Assessment of labelling obligations and rollout of the required disclosures under the current regulation. Prevents fines for insufficient labelling of synthetic content.
Source Data Archive
Handover of a locked archive (prompts, seed values, reference inputs) with a 6-month guarantee for legislative updates. If the rules change, we update the metadata in master files free of charge.
